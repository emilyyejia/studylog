About the Math Problem Collector

This app is like your personal mistake notebook for math. You can save math problems you find difficult, organize them with tags, and add translations in different languages. It’s made for learners from all backgrounds, supporting multicultural and multilingual understanding. By using translations and tags, you can learn math in a way that fits you best. Keep track of your challenges, learn from your mistakes, and improve your math skills — all in one place!
